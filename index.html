Little Profile

I am not a newbie in the academic World. To be where I am today requires a lot of works and enthusiasms. Many work had been put in place since primary school to JSS span to SSS level.

I was the best Speaker of the 1995/1996 Academy Year of the Ahmadiyya Muslim Secondary School (AMSS) Kissy Dock Yard, FREETOWN. During this tenure, I initiated the idea for female pupils to wear veil when in uniform; this scheme was able to create a distinction and soon other Muslim schools emulated. Also, I won three (3) computers and a printer during the speaker contest organised by the Islamic Confederation. The computers and printer were used to set up the first computer school at AMSS- 1996/1997 Academic Year.
